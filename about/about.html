About me

I am a recent graduate of the University of California, Santa Barbara, with a BS in Mathematical Sciences and a BA in English. I am currently an operations research analyst for the United States Air Force and pursuing an MS in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University. My research interests are natural language generation, virtual reality, and modeling and simulation. The aesthetic for this blog is inspired by my love for the 80s, vaporwave, retrowave, and synthwave.

Special thanks for Luke Craig for inspiring the style of this blog.
