About me

I am a recent graduate from the Johns Hopkins University with an MS in Computer Science, and I hold a BS in Mathematical Sciences and a BA in English from the University of California, Santa Barbara. I am currently a software developer for the United States Air Force. My research interests are natural language generation, virtual reality, and modeling and simulation.

I initially started this blog as a way to host the ARM immediate value encoding ... for dummies article for future students (the post is also linked on my professor's website). However, the blog has now grown into a living document for my ongoing projects. Plus, it is a great resume builder. The aesthetic for this blog is inspired by my love for the 80s, vaporwave, retrowave, synthwave, and cyberpunk.

Special thanks for Luke Craig for inspiring the style of this blog.
