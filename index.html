ARM immediate value encoding ... for dummies

11-11-2021

Introduction

During the first term of my M.S. in Computer Science program at Johns Hopkins, I took Computer Architecture with Dr. Kann. He had a list of final projects that we could do, one of which was to develop an interactive tool to teach students about the immediate Operand2 encoding that was available on the ARM chips. This blog is intended to be a companion to Alisdair McDiarmid's excellent blog post on the same topic. The only difference being that the interactive tool presented here goes into much more detail on the steps taken to encode a number.
